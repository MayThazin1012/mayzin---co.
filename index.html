May Zin & Co.

May Zin & Co. is a distinguished Fine Jewelry Atelier dedicated to crafting pieces of enduring beauty and exceptional quality. Defined by an unwavering commitment to artisanal excellence, our collections fuse classic silhouettes with subtle, modern refinement. We meticulously select the finest materials to create future heirlooms for the discerning collector who values master craftsmanship and an aesthetic that transcends passing trends.

Incredible Collection

The Mysterious Raindrops is a signature line of High Jewelry that captures the ephemeral beauty of water and light. Inspired by cloudbursts, shimmering tides, and the graceful movement of drops, this collection is defined by its fluid, sculptural forms and a captivating palette of blue and white.
Each piece features meticulously hand-finished white enamel or mother-of-pearl contrasted with waves of pave-set sapphires and diamonds. The defining feature is the use of highly polished, custom-cut blue gemstones (like lapis, chalcedony, or sapphire) carved into elongated, kinetic drop shapes, designed to mimic the weight and movement of actual water.
This is crafted for the sophisticated collector who seeks jewelry that is both intensely beautiful and deeply evocative, embodying a unique blend of opulent artistry and natural poetry.

The Powerful Simple is a signature line of Haute Joaillerie that redefines minimalist luxury through maximum diamond impact. Inspired by the concept of effortless grandeur and pure light, this is characterized by its streamlined elegance and the dynamic interplay of various diamond cuts.
Each piece features immaculately set, high-clarity white diamonds—including brilliant, baguette, emerald, and pear cuts—arranged in architectural yet fluid compositions. The designs maintain a clean, monochromatic palette in platinum or white gold, allowing the intrinsic fire and sparkle of the diamonds to command attention.
This collection is crafted for the discerning connoisseur who understands that subtlety can be the most potent form of luxury, seeking jewelry that is powerfully elegant, unequivocally brilliant, and timeless in its simplicity.

The AURA Collection by May Zin & Co. is the ultimate expression of our Haute Joaillerie artistry, defined by transformative luxury and unparalleled craftsmanship. This exclusive, limited-edition collection moves beyond mere ornamentation, crafting wearable sculptures that embody powerful narratives from the natural and cosmic world.
Each unique piece—from the serene drama of water to the explosive brilliance of light and the rich texture of botanical life—is realized through a rare fusion of traditional techniques, such as intricate sculpting and specialized gem cutting, with vibrant, bespoke materials like enamel and carved precious stones.
The AURA Collection is created for the collector who seeks a singular, powerful statement: jewelry that possesses an indefinable, magnetic presence and is destined to become a generational heirloom.

The Incredible Collection by May Zin & Co. is a profoundly intimate exploration of Natural Wonders and Personal Journeys. This collection features one-of-a-kind and extremely limited-edition pieces, each centered around a collector-grade, ethically-sourced gemstone (such as a rare Kashmir Sapphire or Paraiba Tourmaline). Crafted with an emphasis on fluid, nature-inspired settings in 18k Fair-mined Gold, the Incredible Collection serves as an investment in legacy—jewelry that is defined not just by its rarity, but by the extraordinary story embedded in every facet and curve.
